[b]Full Mineral Names[/b]

Converts all instances of a mineral's symbol into their full mineral name. 

Used in places like the mineral market, cargo bay and processed mineral displays, and Geologist identification markers.

Also supports minerals added by: [i]Hev's More Minerals[/i]
